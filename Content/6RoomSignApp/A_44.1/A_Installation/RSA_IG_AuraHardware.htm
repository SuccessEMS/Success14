Aura Hardware for the EMS Room Sign App

Aura hardware is interactive digital signage that can be used with the EMS Room Sign App to enhance the user experience. This section will detail the process of preparing an Aura device for EMS connectivity with the EMS Room Sign App.

There are two options for Aura hardware integration with the EMS Room Sign App:

  1. Direct authentication
  2. Server-to-server authentication

Prerequisites for Aura Hardware

Customers must have Update 19 or above of the EMS Room Sign App installed.

Direct Integration

Direct integration allows customers to use the EMS Room Sign App on the Aura device. The Aura hardware is configured in the EMS Desktop Client:

    1. Create a new profile for your device in the EMS Desktop Client.
    2. Navigate to Settings > Misc > Select Hardware Enclosure.

    1. From the drop-down, select Aura.

    Note: If this selection is not made, the LEDs on your device will not change color based on status.

    1. Click OK. Close the profile to Save.
  2. Turn on the Aura device.
  3. Perform initial device setup.
  4. Enter your EMS Room Sign App URL.
  5. You will be prompted to select a profile. If this is not your initial integration with your Aura device, you will need to double click the EMS icon to select a profile.
  6. Click Apply.

Additional information

LED integration

The color of the LEDs on the Aura device will indicate the status of the room you are booking:

  1. Green light = Available room
  2. Red = Booked room
  3. Orange = Available to check in
