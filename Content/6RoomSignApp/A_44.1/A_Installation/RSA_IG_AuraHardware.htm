Aura Hardware for the EMS Room Sign App

Aura hardware is interactive digital signage that can be used with the EMS Room Sign App. Aura hardware offers both LED and Radio Frequency Identification Devices/Barcoding (RFID) solutions that will enhance the EMS Room Sign App user experience. This section will detail the process of preparing an Aura device for EMS connectivity with the EMS Room Sign App.

Prerequisites for Aura Hardware

Users must have the most recent version of the EMS Room Sign App installed. The Aura hardware is configured in the EMS Desktop Client:

  1. Create a new profile for your Aura hardware in the EMS Desktop Client.
  2. Navigate to Settings > Misc > Select Hardware Enclosure.
  3. From the drop-down, select Aura.
  4. Click OK. Close the profile to Save.

There are two options for Aura hardware authentication:

  1. Direct authentication
  2. Server-to-server authentication

Direct Authentication

  1. Turn on the Aura device.
  2. To unlock the screen, enter your password and click Unlock.
  3. Set up your network. Ensure that the content field is set to XXX. This content field cannot be changed.
  4. Click on the Wifi tab and choose the network that the EMS Room Sign App is installed on.
  5. Click Play. You will be taken to a configuration screen.
  6. Starting from the left side of the screen, touch the four corners of the screen. Enter your password again and click OK.
  7. Click the three dots on the top, upper right-hand corner of the screen. Click the screen URL.
  8. Enter your EMS Room Sign App URL.
  9. Configure the Watchdog Timer Interval Default. The default value is 0.
  10. You will be prompted to select a profile. If this is not your initial authentication with your Aura device, you will need to double click the EMS icon to select a profile.
  11. Click Apply.

Authentication Types

You have two options for authentication types with your Aura device:

  1. LED integration—The color of the LEDs on the Aura device will indicate the status of the room you are booking:
    1. Green light = Available room
    2. Red = Booked room
    3. Orange = Available to check in
  2. RFID (badge swipe) integration— In order to work with the EMS Room Sign App, the built-in RFID card reader must be configured to convert access codes to a decimal output. This is a simple Android settings change that must be done per device:
    1. Navigate to the Settings tab > Advanced settings.
    2. Click on the More option.
    3. Ensure the Nfc card uuid data format is set to Decimal.
